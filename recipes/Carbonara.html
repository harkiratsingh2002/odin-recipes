Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. This simple yet indulgent recipe comes together in under 30 minutes, offering a creamy and savory meal that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or guanciale (Italian cured meat)
  • Eggs (preferably large eggs)
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper (freshly ground)
  • Salt
  • Olive oil (optional, for sautéing)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat a pan over medium heat and sauté the pancetta or guanciale in olive oil until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan, and Pecorino Romano. Add a pinch of black pepper.
  4. Once the pasta is done, reserve some pasta water and drain the rest. Add the hot pasta directly into the pan with pancetta and mix to coat.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, adding reserved pasta water if necessary to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra cheese and pepper.
